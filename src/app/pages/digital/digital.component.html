Paid Marketing

Paid digital marketing refers to when a company or brand pays to promote on various digital channels (mainly social media and Google). Paid search is an effective way to raise brand recognition and reach out to new prospective clients at the start of their buying cycle. You may use Google AdWords to target certain phrases and audiences, as well as tell Google how much you want to pay for clicks and how much money you want to spend. Paid social media marketing may be used to reach out to new consumers who share similar interests as your present followers, or to increase the visibility of your current material, as certain social media platforms, such as Facebook, limit organic reach.

Organic marketing

Organic marketing is any type of digital marketing that does not rely on sponsored advertisements. It includes channels such as video sharing, influencers, and SEO. It is most typically associated with social media and content. Organic marketing pushes traffic to the top of your sales funnel during the “awareness” stage, but it also plays a role during the “consideration” stage, when buyers are weighing their alternatives or learning more about your product before making a purchase decision. It fosters an emotional bond between your customers and your business, which is the most important factor in today's great brand experience. Organic marketing tactics aim to build an emotional connection with your consumers through informative or entertaining content, putting your company in front of their minds when they're ready to buy.

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

The act of altering your website so that it can be accessed by search engines is known as search engine optimisation (SEO). It belongs to the organic digital marketing sector. Even though the Google algorithm is so complex and constantly changing, SEO strategies such as technical website optimisation, content marketing, and even some social media are used. Through keyword research and white hat SEO strategies, we can help you get higher organic rankings and more visibility.

Search Engine Marketing(SEM)

SEM (search engine marketing) is a type of digital marketing that aims to boost a website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs). SEM overlaps with search engine optimization (SEO) in that it can encompass strategies such as changing a website's content and architecture to improve ranks. SEM is a form of a digital marketing strategy aimed at placing your business or customer at the top of the first page. The idea is to increase the number of people who visit your website and then convert them into customers.
