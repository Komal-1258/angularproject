Logo & Brand Identity

A logo is not the same as your brand or company identity. Logo design, identity design, and branding all play diverse roles in forming a company's or product's perceived image. A logo is a mark, flag, symbol, or signature that is used to identify a company or product. A logo does not immediately sell a firm, and it seldom describes one. The quality of the item a logo represents determines its meaning, not the other way around — logos are there to identify, not to explain. To summarise, a logo's meaning is more important than its look. Your logo, as part of your brand's identification, should assist market your business and strengthen your brand's identity.Logos can become increasingly simple over time, transitioning from a complicated graphic to a more basic, yet distinct image.
